About me

Adventurere, Artist, Photographer &
Conservationist Trying to make a differance

In the heart of Africa lies a realm where the untouched landscapes of Namibia whisper stories of raw, untamed beauty. Welcome to Leopard Eye Photography, where the lens becomes a storyteller, and each frame captures the essence of this extraordinary land. I am Roy, an explorer, photographer, and custodian of Namibia's secrets. Here, we embark on a visual odyssey that transcends mere documentation. It's a journey into the sublime, where the vastness of deserts, the majesty of mountains, and the untold narratives of wildlife blend to form a canvas of unparalleled magnificence. Through the lens, I invite you to witness the primal allure of Namibia, a country that has etched its indomitable spirit onto every frame.
