Culture & Community

The Diverse and Extraordinary
People of Namibia

Namibia's indigenous people have forged lives amidst this hostile and unforgiving land, their cultural heritage as fascinating as the landscape they've learned to coexist with and call home. The Ovahimba of northern Namibia are an incredible people, living in unity with nature amidst the perils of this unpredictable environment. Every aspect of their daily life is altered to adapt to an ecosystem where rainfall is as unpredictable as a wounded buffalo.
